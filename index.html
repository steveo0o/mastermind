Logical Hint

How to Play

Objective

Guess the secret code of 4 colored pegs within 10 attempts.

How to Play

  1. Click on empty circles in a row to select them
  2. Click colors from the bottom to fill selected circles
  3. Click "CHECK" to submit your guess
  4. Get feedback with red and white pegs

Feedback Pegs

Red = Correct color in correct position
White = Correct color in wrong position

Tips

  • Toggle "Repeats" to allow/disallow duplicate colors
  • Use the hint button when stuck for logical guidance
  • Pay attention to patterns between attempts
